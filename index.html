Who I am

Software Engineer | Backend Developer

I currently work at CA Technologies as a Backend Java Developer. I mainly use web frameworks that utilize the Spring Projects such as Spring Boot and Spring Data. Building REST APIs is something I do on a regular basis and DevOps is a new field of work for me. I am familiar with AWS and setting up Kubernetes Clusters for various types of projects.

I am a very active software engineer. Not your usual engineering type, as you can find me rock climbing, playing in beach volleyball tournaments, playing guitar or snowboarding.
